## Tweet 2 — Deliverability Protocol
**URL:** https://x.com/coldemailchris/status/2053170063994171673
**Date:** 9 May 2026 | 24 likes

Protocol across every client:

- **1:1 inbox ratio** — 100 sending inboxes + 100 on warmup reserve, rotate monthly
- Replace disconnected inboxes every month without fail
- **Warmup:** start at 5/day, increase by 1 email per day until 20
- **Cold ramp:** start at 5/day post-warmup, increase by 1/day
- **Throttle check** at every step — watch reply rates + bounce rates as you scale
- **Steady state:** 10 emails/day per inbox for longevity (20 if campaigns are crushing)
- **Google over Microsoft, every time**

Without this in place campaigns die before they ever had a chance.

## BONUS — AI Cold Email Copy Prompt (13 May 2026)
**URL:** https://x.com/coldemailchris/status/2054359491408273739

RevGrowth's actual AI prompt specs:
- **Length cap:** < 70 words (target 30 / 45 / 60 word bands)
- **Tone:** Short, conversational, professional — not slangy
- **Zero fluff:** Every sentence must earn its place
- **CTA:** Soft/value-based question only ("Worth a quick look?", "Open to exploring?")
- **Subject lines:** 1-3 words, no punctuation, spintax format
- **3 complexity tiers:** Simple / Niche-aware / Hyper-specific
- **3 lengths:** ~30 / ~45 / ~60 words
- **Structural elements (at least 3 per script):**
  - Personalized Hook (8-12 words)
  - Social Proof Bridge (15-20 words)
  - Value Proposition (10-15 words)
  - Front-End Offer (8-12 words)
  - Soft CTA (5-8 words)
- **Variables:** {{first_name}}, {{company_name}}, {{recent_news}}, {{tech_stack}}, {{hiring_signal}}, {{competitor_touch}}, {{peer_company}}, {{pqs_trigger}}
- **Guardrails:** 25-69 words strict, Clay-merge-safe variables, no emojis, no punctuation in subjects
